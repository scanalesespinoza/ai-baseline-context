# Workflows

Pick the playbook that matches your task.

- `production_fix_cycle.md` – end-to-end bugfix to production, with planning and verification.
- `full_release_cycle.md` – release creation and merge with auto-deploy.
- `production_verification.md` – post-deploy validation steps.
- `repository_health_check.md` – quick health audit of a repo.
- `verify_community_join.md` – checklist for community join/onboarding flow.
