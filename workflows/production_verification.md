---
description: Verify the production deployment of the Homedir platform
---

This workflow performs a visual and functional verification of the key pages on the production site.

1. **Navigate to Production**:
   - URL: `https://homedir.opensourcesantiago.io/`

2. **Verify Home Page**:
   - Check that the "HomeDir" logo is visible.
   - Verify the main sections (Community, Projects, Events) are present.

3. **Verify Community Page**:
   - Click "Comunidad" in the nav bar.
   - Verify the "Participantes" list is loaded and visible.

4. **Verify Projects Page**:
   - Click "Proyectos" in the nav bar.
   - Verify the projects grid or board is visible.

5. **Verify Events Page**:
   - Click "Eventos" in the nav bar.
   - Verify the "Eventos y charlas" header.
   - **Crucial**: Check the "Hoy" card displays the current date correctly (e.g., `YYYY-MM-DD`).

6. **General Health Check**:
   - Look for any broken images or 404 errors during navigation.
   - Click the "Login" button to ensure the modal or login flow triggers (no need to complete login).
